#📊 Decision Making with Business Statistics

##📌 Project Overview
This project demonstrates how statistical methods can be used to support driven decision-making across different real-world scenarios. This analysis applies statistical techniques such as probability theory, normal distribution analysis, hypothesis testing, and ANOVA to understand patterns and relationships in data.

The project explores four different domains:
- Sports analytics
- Manufacturing quality control
- Material hardness evaluation
- Healthcare process analysis

By applying statistical tools to these problems, the project illustrates how data can guide better operational and business decisions.

The main objectives of this project are:
- Apply probability concepts to analyze real-world scenarios
- Use statistical distributions to estimate probabilities

##📈 Problem Analysis
###1️⃣ Foot Injuries and Player Positions
A physiotherapist wanted to understand whether foot injuries among football players are related to their playing positions.
The analysis evaluates:
- Probability of a player suffering an injury
- Probability of a player being a forward or winger
- Probability that a striker is injured
- Conditional probability of an injured player being a striker.

Dataset Used: data/football_injury_data.csv

###2️⃣ Breaking Strength of Gunny Bags
A cement company evaluated the strength of gunny bags used for packaging. Weak bags could lead to material loss during transportation.
The strength follows a normal distribution with:
- Mean = 5 kg/cm²
- Standard Deviation = 1.5 kg/cm²
The analysis calculates probabilities for different strength ranges.

###3️⃣ Zingaro Stone Hardness Analysis
Zingaro printing requires stones with a minimum Brinell hardness index of 150.
The study compares unpolished stones and treated & polished stones to determine whether polishing improves hardness.
Statistical methods used:
- Descriptive statistics
- Histogram analysis
- One-sample t-test
- Two-sample t-test
The analysis confirms that **unpolished stones are not suitable for printing without treatment**, while the polishing process significantly improves stone hardness and makes them more suitable for the printing process.

###4️⃣ Dental Implant Hardness Analysis
This study evaluated how implant hardness varies depending on dentist, method, and alloy type.
Statistical techniques used:
- One-way ANOVA
- Two-way ANOVA
- Interaction analysis
The results indicate that implant hardness is influenced more by the method used than by the dentist alone.
